Description

A rolling 12-bar shuffle in the key of E — the backbone groove for half the blues canon. The whole thing lives in the low strings with a swung eighth-note feel.

Focus areas while learning:

  • Keep the right-hand palm mute consistent through the whole bar.
  • The reach from the open chord to the 4th-fret “walk-up” is the hard part — isolate it.
  • Nail the turnaround in bars 11–12 before pushing tempo.

Goal: play it clean and relaxed at 120 BPM with a real swing feel, not a stiff straight-eighth version.
